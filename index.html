Trip On Track – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Trip On Track collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when using our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address
  • Trip information and preferences
  • Expense and budget details
  • Location data for weather and travel suggestions
  • Device and analytics information

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide travel planning tools
  • Track expenses and budgets
  • Generate weather insights
  • Enable group trip management
  • Improve app performance and security

3. Location Access

Location access is used only for travel recommendations and weather forecasting. You can disable location permission anytime from device settings.

4. Group Trips & QR Access

Users can join trips using QR codes or invite codes. Users are responsible for sharing access only with trusted members.

5. Third-Party Services

Trip On Track may use trusted third-party services including analytics providers, cloud storage, authentication systems, and weather APIs.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable security practices to protect user information. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security.

7. Children's Privacy

Trip On Track is not intended for children under 13 years old.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app means acceptance of the updated policy.
